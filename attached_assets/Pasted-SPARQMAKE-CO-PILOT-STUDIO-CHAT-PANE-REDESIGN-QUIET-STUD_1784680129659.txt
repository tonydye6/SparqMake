artifacts/sparqmake/src/pages/CopilotStudio.tsx (~1800 lines) contains the entire
Co-pilot Studio: HomeView, StatusBadge, a CHIPS array, sessionReducer, SessionView
(thread + composer dock + preview), FanOutCard, TurnCard, and the root component.

The session view currently stacks up to six control rows above the composer: 5 action
chips, an Edit region chip, an Edit video chip, a 7-pill "Caption for:" row, region
banners, an inline asset picker panel, and attach chips. The thread is fixed at
w-[420px]; the preview image is capped at max-w-sm (384px); turn thumbnails are 64px

single verb surface (slash commands + @asset mentions, Enter sends). Next-step
suggestions render on the turn card that earned them. Controls move onto the objects
they belong to: region select onto the image, platform targeting and rewrites onto a
caption card. The thread becomes resizable and collapsible. Nothing becomes unreachable.

  error toast (the wasAborted branch).

- Session persistence in the URL (?session=) incl. the consume-once guard, and the
  ContentPlan deep-link (?campaign= + ?platform=) seeding flow.

  local-datetime helper (no UTC drift).

  /edit <instruction>  -> runTurn("edit_image", ...). Needs imageInteractionId.
